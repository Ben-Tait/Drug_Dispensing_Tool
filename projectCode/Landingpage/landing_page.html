How It Works

  1. Patients register on the website and request prescriptions from doctors.
  2. Doctors review and approve prescription requests, assigning medications to patients.
  3. The pharmacy receives the approved prescriptions and dispenses medications to patients.
  4. A pharmaceutical company supplies drugs to the pharmacy, ensuring an adequate stock of medications.
